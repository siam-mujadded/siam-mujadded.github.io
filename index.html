Research

Effect of Emotional Expressions on Facial Landmark-Based Face Recognition

Authors: Utchchhwas Singha, Kamruj Jaman Sheam, Siam Al Mujadded, Ashikur Rahman

Journal: Inderscience Publishers (Accepted for publication)

Abstract:

Investigated the impact of six primary emotional expressions (happiness, sadness, anger, disgust, surprise, fear) on the accuracy of facial landmark-based face recognition systems. Proposed novel emotion-specific geometric features derived from 68 facial landmarks to enhance recognition accuracy in resource-constrained environments.

Conducted experiments using the CK+ dataset with multiple machine learning models; demonstrated that combining neutral and emotion-specific features significantly improves accuracy (LDA achieving up to 99.98% for certain emotions).

Concluded that emotional traits should be integrated into recognition algorithms to improve robustness in dynamic, real-world applications such as surveillance and real-time identity verification.

Supervisor: Prof. Ashikur Rahman, BUET
